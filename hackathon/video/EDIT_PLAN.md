## Purpose

Produce an English demo under three minutes that shows JAS through the working
Taqueria Viva application and explains the development-time role of Codex SOL
and GPT-5.6.

- Original recording: `taqueria viva demo hackathon.webm`
- Original duration: approximately 84 minutes
- Original audio: none

sections from the development workflow and the functional application demo.

## Narrative structure

1. Introduce the operational problem and Taqueria Viva.
2. Show Codex working under the repository's `AGENTS.md` protocol.
3. Explain honestly how Codex SOL and GPT-5.6 participated during development.
4. State that PHP, JAS and DataCore run independently from AI services.
5. Demonstrate login, role separation, catalog, kitchen, inventory, cash and
   reports.
6. Explain governed validation and DataCore's authoritative role.
7. Close by identifying JAS as the hackathon project and Taqueria Viva as its
   application demonstration.
